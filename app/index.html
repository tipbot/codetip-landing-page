A simple and easy way to show  your appreciation for people on GitHub.

Codetip allows you to send lumens to anyone with a GitHub account.

What is Codetip?

A great way to show your appreciation for people on GitHub! It allows you to send lumens to anyone with a GitHub account.

It monitors comments for any mention of @tipbot.

What are lumens?

Lumens are the native asset of the Stellar network.

Native means that lumens are built into the network. Asset is how the network refers to an item of value that is stored on the ledger.

One lumen is a unit of digital currency, like a bitcoin.

While you can’t hold a lumen in your hand, they are essential to the Stellar network—they contribute to the ability to move money around the world and to conduct transactions between different currencies quickly and securely.

How does it work?

  •                     @tipbot
    Send the default tip of 200 lumens to the creator of the issue you are commenting on. 
  •                   @tipbot <amount>
    Send a custom amount of lumens to the creator of the issue you are commenting on. 
  •                     @tipbot <GitHub name>
    Send the default tip of 200 lumens to the specified GitHub user. 
  •                     @tipbot <amount> <GitHub name>
    Send a custom amount of lumens to the specified GitHub user. 
  •                     @tipbot empty <payment address or account ID>
    Clear your tipbot account and send all the lumens in it to some other account.
Example of tipbot in use
You can also send directly to any GitHub account directly from your Stellar wallet by sending to: 
<GitHub_name>*codetip.io
. For example you can send to: 
irisli*codetip.io
.
